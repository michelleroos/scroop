composting storing food IPCC report

did you know that up to 40% of the food supply in the US goes wasted every year and that food waste makes up almost 20% of the overall waste stream? what's worse is that our landfills are so packed that food is left to biodegrade virtually without any oxygen. this process produces a dangerous greenhouse gas called methane. over a span of 20-30 years, methane is between 20-150 times more harmful to the environment than carbondioxide.

scroople was created to help you search for recipes using ingredients. that way, you can search using ingredients you already have at home or that you found at your local food market.

here's how it works

  1. enter the ingredients you would like to search with in the search bar on the home page (separate them using commas)
  2. use the 'i'm feeling lucky' button if you want to view recipes from a randomly selected diet instead
  3. on the next page, click 'tools' to filter your results
  4. that's it!
